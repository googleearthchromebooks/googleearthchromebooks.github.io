System Status (Windows Apps)

The status of the system serving Windows apps is color-coded to let you quickly check if you can run them.

  • Green: The system is ready
    All systems are operational and ready to go.
  • Orange: The system will be available shortly
    The system is not available at the moment, but should be available shortly (in most cases, the system is powering off or starting).
  • Red: The system is offline
    All resources are offline. Ask your administrator to turn the system on.

Note: This indicator affects only Windows apps. Web apps will be available at all times.

Help

Here’s a quick list of tips you can use to make the most out of using your Launchpad:

- Check system status (upper right corner):
---> Green: the system is ready
---> Orange: the system will be ready in a couple of minutes
---> Red: your admin needs to add capacity
- Resize your browser and the app will scale automatically
- The 5 circles in the lower left corner show your network status
- Launch other apps from the gears menu in the lower left corner
- "Disconnect" keeps your session active
- "Quit" or "Power off" will end your session
